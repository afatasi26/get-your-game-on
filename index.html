About Us

We here at Get Your Game On, are simple everyday people, who just happen to be sports fans. We provide a website for people like us to find Professional sports games in any major city in the United States. You can search any city with our site and find upcoming games in that city, or the nearest city to your search who has a professional team. If you happen to be traveling in the upcoming future, then let us help you with finding live sports games for you and your friends and family to enjoy.

How To Start

You may be asking yourself "How do I get started", no worries we are here to help. Simply enter the city you are interested in, click search and let the magic happen. (BOOM) just like that you have listings of upcoming games, times, locations and links to where you can purchase tickets. Now that you know how to get started, search the city of your next travel destination and (Get Your Game On).
